# 06_logic.md — Brenda & Steve Catalano — Off-Ramp Shapes

This file is intentionally NOT exhaustive. It encodes the strategic shapes
the Liaison Agent should recognize, not every literal branch.

## On every reply from Brenda or Steve

- 🟢 Brenda confirms tasting attendance / sends venue name
  → confirm immediately. Lock the headcount. Note venue for logistics.
- 🟢 Brenda asks an operational question (dietary, timing, parking, kids)
  → answer with precision. Ask the dietary question back to her if anyone in the party has restrictions she hasn't named.
- 🟢 Brenda asks to bring more than 10 people
  → say yes immediately. Never haggle headcount.
- 🔵 **Steve emails directly** (any form, any signature)
  → STRONGEST possible signal. Reply within 30 minutes. Tighter, operational language. Treat it like a B2B email. Tell Andre to read it himself before any reply goes out.
- 🟢 They ask about pricing pre-tasting
  → don't quote. Defer with "let's eat first Sunday, talk numbers after."
- 🟢 They mention the daughter or fiancé
  → pivot toward her. Daughter has veto power.
- 🔵 They mention Tennessee operations
  → SLOW DOWN. This is the year-two relationship door. Acknowledge once ("I'd love to hear about that someday"), refocus on the wedding. Do NOT auto-respond as if this is normal small talk — it isn't.
- 🟡 They cancel or reschedule the tasting
  → say yes immediately. Offer a private weekday tasting as the recovery move. This is rescuable.
- 🔴 "Stop contacting me" / hostile
  → pause cadence permanently, log Lost-with-reason in Close.

## Plan-redesign triggers (rare — scrap the plan, don't branch within it)

- Steve emails directly with a substantive question or request.
- Daughter or fiancé engages directly (any reply not from Brenda or Steve).
- They flip to a non-Brazilian cuisine vision.
- They name a competing caterer.
- They ask Andre to commit to a price, contract, or scope before the tasting.

## On silence by checkpoint

- 🟡 Silent by Day 3 → execute Day 4 SMS to Brenda as planned.
- 🟡 Silent by Day 5 → execute Day 6 pre-tasting confirmation email regardless. The Day 6 email goes out whether they replied or not.
- 🟡 Silent through Day 7 → Saturday morning single SMS: "see you tomorrow at 5:30 — we're set up for you."
- 🔴 They no-show on May 3rd
  → Monday: Andre CALLS Brenda (does not text). Warm, no-blame. Find out what happened.

## Hard rules

- ❌ Generic NEPQ template language. Steve reads templates for breakfast.
- ❌ "Understanding Our Catering Service Types" or "Churrasco vs Rodizio" emails. Already paused. Never re-enable.
- ❌ Chasing the tasting fee. Already waived. Done.
- ❌ Quoting price before the tasting.
- ❌ Pitching the dessert table upsell out loud. They already have the offer; saying it cheapens it.
