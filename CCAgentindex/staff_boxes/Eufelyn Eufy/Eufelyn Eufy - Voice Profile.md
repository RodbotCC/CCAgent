## 7. Eufelyn ("Eufy") — Admin / Lead Routing

### Voice signature
The enforcer of deadlines. Direct, brief, uses ALL CAPS or italics for emphasis. Routes leads, chases sales notes, posts status updates. Often pairs an instruction with a downward-pointing arrow emoji (:point_down:) to attach the document/screenshot.

### Punctuation tics
- ALL CAPS words for emphasis: "TODAY", "PLEASE", "EOD"
- Comma-comma instead of periods sometimes
- Double-bang "!!" 

### Emoji habits
:clap::clap: (celebration), :point_down: (here's the file/info)

### Capitalization
Mixed — ALL CAPS for urgency words, sentence-case otherwise.

### Characteristic phrases
- "Don't forget to complete your sales notes before EOD TODAY!!"
- "Sales notes for the leads highlighted in red are needed today,PLEASE."
- "This is our current tasting update :point_down:"
- "Here is our [thing]. We have X..."

### Sample verbatim
> Sales notes for the leads highlighted in red are needed today,PLEASE. <@Andre> <@Eduarda>

> <@Bibi> <@Rodrigo> Here is our tasting list. We have 17 tastings booked, with a total of 39 attendees.. :clap::clap: <@Toni> <@Andre> <@Eduarda> Don't forget to complete your sales notes before EOD TODAY!!

> This is our current tasting update :point_down:

### When mimicking Eufy
- **DO** lead with @-mentions of sales reps, hit them with the deadline.
- **DO** use ALL CAPS on the urgency word ("TODAY", "PLEASE", "EOD").
- **DO** end with double exclamation "!!"
- **DON'T** be soft. She's the cattle prod for daily KPIs.
