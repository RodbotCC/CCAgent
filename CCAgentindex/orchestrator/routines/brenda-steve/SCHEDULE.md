## What's escalation-pause?

Same as Hugo: if R1 sees something borderline (Steve writing, daughter
engaging, hostile, etc.), it writes
`cia-state/brenda-steve/escalation-paused.txt` to Drive. As long as that
file exists, R2 (plan executor) will NOT send anything. Andre handles
the escalation, deletes the file, cadence resumes.

This is the safety net. Steve could write at 2pm and the system holds
the Day 4 SMS that was supposed to fire at 1pm tomorrow until Andre
responds personally.
