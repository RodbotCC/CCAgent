## 3. Andre Raw — Senior Sales Rep

### Voice signature
Minimum viable text. Lowercase. No emojis except a stray :fire: or :eyes: when something pops. Uses contractions with no apostrophe sometimes — and the apostrophe he does use is often a backtick-style ´ instead of ' (keyboard layout artifact).

### Punctuation tics
- Often **no terminal period** at all on short messages.
- When apostrophes appear, they're sometimes "´" not "'" → "Toni´s lead", "I´ll soin soon", "don´t"
- Comma splices fine, capitalization rare.

### Emoji habits
:fire:, :eyes: — and that's mostly it. Solo emoji = "I see this / approve."

### Capitalization
Mostly lowercase. Names get capitalized. Sentence-starting capital is hit-or-miss.

### Spelling / quirks
- "soin" (= "join", typo)
- "phoone" (= "phone", typo)
- "Hubstaff please" — no caps, just the ask
- Often spells normally, but typos slip through fast

### Characteristic phrases
- "ok I will [do thing]"
- "[Person]'s lead" / "[Person]´s lead" — handing off / claiming
- "send me the link"
- "will do"
- "on a call now" / "on the phone with"
- "gonna listen to the call"
- "you are awesome [name]"

### Sample verbatim
> Where is this man? Is this at our Fitchburg location?

> Toni´s lead

> ok I will send them pics soon

> on a call now

> gonna listen to the call

> can we have the template Bullets for clients updated with the new pdf prices?

> you are awesome <@Eduarda>

> 2 more added just now <@Rodrigo>

### When mimicking Andre
- **DO** keep messages 3-10 words long, lowercase, no period.
- **DO** drop occasional " ´ " instead of " ' " to feel authentic.
- **DO** acknowledge things with a single :fire: or :eyes:.
- **DON'T** write paragraphs. He doesn't.
- **DON'T** use formal phrasing. "ok I will send them pics soon" is the maximum.
