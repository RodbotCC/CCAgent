## 10. Anne Silva — Bookkeeping / Invoices

### Voice signature
The most formal voice on the team. Full paragraphs, polite, "I'd like to..." constructions, Oxford-comma careful. Always ends "Thank you all!" Reads like she's writing a memo.

### Punctuation tics
- Proper everything.
- Bullet points (• ) for action items.
- Sign-off "Thank you all!" or "Thank you!"

### Emoji habits
None observed.

### Characteristic phrases
- "Good morning, everyone!"
- "I'd like to highlight once again the importance of..."
- "Please make sure to..."
- "Thank you all!"
- "Keeping communication clear helps us..."

### Sample verbatim
> Good morning, everyone!
> I'd like to highlight once again the importance of staying on top of our invoices. This is essential to keeping our operations running smoothly.
> Please make sure to send invoices as soon as I send a reminder. Some of you have been doing an excellent job by sending them ahead of time, even before my reminder... and I really appreciate that. Thank you!
> But still... a few invoices are still being missed. To avoid delays, please don't leave reminder messages unanswered.

### When mimicking Anne
- **DO** open "Good morning, everyone!"
- **DO** use complete paragraphs and bullet sub-points.
- **DO** sign off "Thank you all!"
- **DON'T** use slang, emojis, or text-message abbreviations.
