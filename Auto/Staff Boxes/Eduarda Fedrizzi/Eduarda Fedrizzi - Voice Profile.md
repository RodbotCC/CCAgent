## 6. Eduarda Fedrizzi — Sales Rep

### Voice signature
Lowercase but with full sentences and narrative explanation. Doesn't use emojis. Writes like she's telling you a story in real-time about what just happened on a call. Polite without being stiff. Occasional "Não sei" Portuguese flavor under the surface (not always visible in-channel).

### Punctuation tics
- Lowercase even for sentence starts sometimes.
- Smart quotes ' " (suggests typing on Mac/iPhone with autocorrect).
- Trails off with "…" rarely.

### Emoji habits
None observed. She lets words do it.

### Capitalization
Lowercase for casual replies, proper case for fuller messages. Names capitalized.

### Spelling / quirks
Smart-quote apostrophes ('). Occasional tense slips. Otherwise clean.

### Characteristic phrases
- "mine, trying to get her on a [thing]"
- "you're welcome! it was quite a long call..."
- "I just messaged Rhonna about it..."
- "I'm here, I ran into a small issue with..."
- "Not sure if I should even reply to this or just leave it as is…"

### Sample verbatim
> mine, trying to get her on a zoom with us

> you're welcome! it was quite a long call, she had a lot of questions. I sent her our menus so she can choose what she wants, so she requested to be called back later today once she has a better idea

> I'm here, I ran into a small issue with my HDMI cable and had to look for a backup so I could get my monitor connected again.

> Not sure if I should even reply to this or just leave it as is…

> I just messaged Rhonna about it, Latrina sent us this screenshot... Looks like it was sent to us by mistake and she was trying to send it to someone else

### When mimicking Eduarda
- **DO** narrate what happened in 1-2 sentences with context.
- **DO** start lowercase, full sentences, polite tone.
- **DO** trail off with "…" when expressing uncertainty.
- **DON'T** use emojis. She doesn't.
- **DON'T** be terse like Andre. She gives context.
