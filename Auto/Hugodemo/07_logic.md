# 07_logic.md — Hugo Casillas — Off-Ramp Shapes

This file is intentionally NOT exhaustive. It encodes the strategic
shapes the Liaison Agent should recognize, not every literal branch.

## On every reply from Hugo

- 🟢 Acknowledges process / asks substantive question
  → respond as a peer, mirror his cadence, answer the question, propose
    the next concrete step in his stated availability window.
- 🟢 Wants the walkthrough video
  → ship it within 4 hours, propose 3 specific weekday call slots within
    5 business days.
- 🔵 Says "send the agreement" / verbal yes / "we're going with you"
  → escalate to Andre + operator. Do NOT auto-send the agreement.
- 🟢 Names a competing quote dollar amount
  → don't match. Ask what's included. Anchor on value gap, not price gap.
- 🔵 Fiancée engages directly (any channel)
  → plan-redesign trigger. Pause 7-day plan, write a fiancée-aware
    branch, escalate to Andre.
- 🔴 "Stop contacting me" / explicit hostile
  → pause cadence permanently, log to Close as Lost-with-reason.
- 🔴 Goes cold past Day 14 from quote (May 1)
  → quote-expiry is the natural reset moment, single message:
    "Quote expires Friday — extend or are you ready to talk?"

## On silence by checkpoint

- 🟡 Silent by Day 3 → execute Day 4 SMS as planned.
- 🟡 Silent by Day 5 → execute Day 6 email as planned.
- 🟡 Silent past Day 7 → pause to Day 14 with one final SMS.

## Plan-redesign triggers (rare — scrap the plan, not branch)

- Fiancée engages directly.
- Hugo asks for in-person non-Sunday tasting.
- Hugo signals close-ready before Day 7.
- Hugo names a competing caterer + their full quote.
