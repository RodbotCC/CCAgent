
## Handoff checklist (universal)

- [ ] Event date in ops calendar
- [ ] Venue logistics confirmed (load-in, power, water, kitchen access)
- [ ] Menu finalized and printed
- [ ] Dietary requirements checked twice
- [ ] Staff scheduled
- [ ] Equipment booked
- [ ] Contact person + phone number on the day
