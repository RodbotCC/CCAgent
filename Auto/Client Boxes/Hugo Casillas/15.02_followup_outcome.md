# 15.02 — Follow-up Outcome (template)

Lead is active but hasn't closed yet. Used when the 7-day plan window
ends without a yes/no, but the lead is still warm.

```
lead_id:
name:
last_active: <date>
next_check_in: <date>
why_still_open: <reason>
plan_when_to_replan: <trigger>
```

## When to use

- Day 7 hits and there's been engagement but no commitment.
- Quote-expiry was extended.
- A specific external event blocks the close (vacation, family event,
  fiscal-quarter-end).

## Cadence

- Light-touch only. One value-add ping every 7–14 days max.
- Reactivate full 7-day plan on a fresh trigger (replied, asked a question,
  named a competitor).
