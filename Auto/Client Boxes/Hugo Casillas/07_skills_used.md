## Skill gaps (skills the plan needs that don't yet exist)

- ❌ **`compose-recovery-message` fiancée variant** — the universal
  recovery skill needs a calibration that targets a non-primary stakeholder
  (the fiancée here). Not built yet. Build trigger: this lead + at least
  one more pair-lead with a vegetarian/dietary-driving secondary.
- ❌ **`compose-quote-cover-email` extension variant** — quote-expiry
  extension is a distinct sub-shape of the cover-email skill. Not yet
  separated. Build trigger: see the pattern twice across leads.
- ❌ **`compose-fiancée-direct` (Tier 1 new)** — if the fiancée engages
  on her own, the response shape is different from any existing Tier 1
  skill. Likely a new skill, not just a calibration. Build trigger: the
  first time it actually happens.

## Tier 2 inheritance

`profile-whale-professional-buyer` calibrates by:
- length: emails get 5–8 real sentences, not bullet-blocks
- urgency: anchored on real deadlines (quote-expiry, venue date), not
  manufactured ones
- voice register: peer-to-peer, never vendor-to-prospect
- forbidden phrases: "circling back", "just checking in", "wanted to touch
  base", any emoji-as-decoration, any !!
- explicit-respect language: "I respect your process" is the wedge
