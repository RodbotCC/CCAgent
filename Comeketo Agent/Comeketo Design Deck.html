04 · States

Six states. One vocabulary, used everywhere.

Comeketo Agent
Observed Inferred Drafted Shipped Stale Blocked
State Means Set by Decay Example
Observed Saw it in the wild — raw signal. Hooks · sensors · intake Becomes Stale after 7d Twitter — scout hour
Inferred Derived by an agent — needs a check. Rodbot · sub-agents Promotes to Drafted on confirm Lead source: FB/IG
Drafted Composed but not committed. Sylvia · operator Stays until promoted/discarded Morning text
Shipped Out the door. Recorded. Operator · Rod Immutable Friday recap
Stale Aged out without a next step. System (timer) Sweeps to archive at 30d Old observed signal
Blocked Cannot proceed. Needs a human. Any actor Cleared on resolve Tagging hygiene gap
Lifecycle
Observed Inferred Drafted Shipped · aging side-paths → Stale · Blocked
